Odisha - Scenic, Serene, Sublime!


Odisha is famous for its classical dance forms, particularly Odissi, one of the oldest dance forms in India. The state is home to numerous temples showcasing exquisite Kalinga architecture, with the Sun Temple at Konark and the Jagannath Temple in Puri being the most notable.The state is known for its vibrant festivals, including the Rath Yatra (Chariot Festival) in Puri, which attracts thousands of devotees. Other significant festivals include Durga Puja, Diwali, and Makar Mela. Odisha features stunning landscapes, from the serene beaches of Puri and Gopalpur to the breathtaking hills of the Eastern Ghats. The Chilika Lake, Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, is a hotspot for migratory birds and offers a unique ecosystem
